Download? But the game's not done.

Yes. The full game is not complete, but as a special treat for the 2023 Mother Direct, the Encore team has been hard at work to finish a demo for any fans or interested watchers to play! We can't thank everyone who's supported this project over the past few years enough for their support, so we hope this shows our gratitude.

For Windows, for Mac, for Linux and beyond, please enjoy Mother Encore!

The demo build is available via the Encore Discord or Github.
